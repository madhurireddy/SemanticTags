Founded in 2004, Facebook’s mission is to give people the power to share and make the world more open and connected.

Google began in March 1995 as a research project by Larry Page and Sergey Brin, Ph.D. students at Stanford University.

Apple was founded by Steven Paul Jobs, Steve Wozniak and Ronald Gerald Wayne on April 1, 1976 and is headquartered in Cupertino, CA.
